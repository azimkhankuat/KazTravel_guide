About Us Page


KazTravel Guide - is a website that helps tourists to plan their trips to Kazakhstan!


Auditory

The main auditory of our website will be tourists that plan to visit our country. And our website will help them to find interesting places of Kazakhstan and will help to plan their visit including where they will live, transport and stuff like that. Also, people of Kazakhstan can also use our website to make a tour inside Kazakhstan, because not all people visited all beautiful places of Kazakhstan. So, in general, all people who enjoy travelling and want to know more about our country can visit our website.
